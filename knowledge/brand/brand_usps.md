## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO Use
- "Transformation" (real, measurable change)
- "Reset" (fresh start, not just relaxation)
- "Restore" (bringing back what was lost)
- "Rebuild" (creating something stronger)
- "Home" (warmth, belonging)
- "Journey" (process, not quick fix)
- "Support" (ongoing care)
- "Root cause" (addressing the real issue)

### DON'T Use
- "Cure" (medical compliance)
- "Luxury spa" (we're more than that)
- "Quick fix" (against our philosophy)
- "Miracle" (we're science-based)
- "Exclusive" (we're accessible)
- "Treatment" alone (always pair with education/support)

## Tone of Voice

### Primary Characteristics
- **Warm**: Like a knowledgeable friend, not a doctor lecturing
- **Confident**: We know what works
- **Educational**: Always explaining the "why"
- **Hopeful**: Change is possible
- **Human**: Real people, real stories, real care

### Voice Examples

**Instead of:**
> "Our facility offers comprehensive detoxification protocols administered by medical professionals."

**Write:**
> "Our team of doctors and wellness experts will guide you through a detox that actually works — and teach you why it works, so you can maintain the results."

**Instead of:**
> "Book your luxury wellness retreat today."

**Write:**
> "Ready to reset? Your transformation starts with a conversation."

## Content Application

When creating content, always ask:
1. Does this reflect "rest, restore, rebuild"?
2. Does this feel warm and human?
3. Are we educating, not just promoting?
4. Does this address root cause, not just symptoms?
5. Is this accessible and welcoming?
6. Does this promise realistic, achievable transformation?
